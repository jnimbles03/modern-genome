Form Analyzer Indicators & Complexity Scoring

Understanding the signals we extract from forms and how we score their difficulty

Key Indicators & Their Meanings

Required Fields (REQ-001)

Identifies fields that should be mandatory (e.g., SSN, DOB, account details) but are marked as optional. Missing required markings raises a high NIGO risk.

SEVERITY: HIGH

Input Masks (FMT-001)

Detects high-risk data fields lacking validation (no input mask or formatting checks). This includes SSN, routing or account numbers, and phone numbers.

SEVERITY: MEDIUM

Signature Date Missing (SIG-002)

Warns when a signature line lacks an adjacent date field. Missing dates can delay processing and trigger follow-up.

SEVERITY: LOW

Conditional Logic Missing (COND-003)

Marks sections referencing beneficiaries or dependents without visible logic to control follow-on questions.

SEVERITY: MEDIUM

Role Unspecified (ROLE-001)

Flags signature fields without a labelled role (Applicant, Co-owner, Notary, etc.). Adding role labels reduces confusion and NIGO risk.

SEVERITY: MEDIUM

Third-Party Coordination (THRD-001)

Highlights when additional parties (Physician, Attorney, Financial Advisor, Guardian) must sign or approve.

SEVERITY: MEDIUM

Attachment Requirements

Looks for text like "attach," "include copy," or mentions of supporting documents. Required attachments add to overall complexity and NIGO risk.

Signatures & Notarization

Counts signature fields and identifies witness and notary requirements. Witness signatures and notarization materially increase form difficulty.

JavaScript / Conditional Logic

Detects embedded JavaScript or multistep logic. Complex logic (branching questions, dynamic sections) signals high cognitive load for signers.

Radio Groups

Counts sets of radio buttons. Multiple radio groups indicate branching choices that may confuse signers and increase complexity.

PII Collection

Scans for sensitive personal data (SSN, DOB, TIN, EIN, account and routing numbers, address, email, phone). Each type of PII adds to the complexity score.

Graphics & Layout

Measures drawings and images per page and text density. Many diagrams or busy pages increase cognitive load and the complexity score.

Deadlines & Dependencies

Extracts deadline mentions ("submit within 30 days") and references to other forms. Each deadline or dependency slightly raises complexity.

Domain Mismatch

Checks whether the hosting domain differs from the issuer/brand. Mismatched domains can signal phishing or confusion and add modest complexity.
